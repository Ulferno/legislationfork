5 F.C. ยง 3 - Site Epsilon

  1. Site Epsilon is the organizations primary entity containment facility within SCI PATHOS III.
  2. Site Epsilons boundary is defined as;
    1. The access port within the Installation Command Moonpool
    2. The manhole access port within SCP-082s containment chamber.
    3. he markation line outside of the Site Epsilon Monorail
    4. The Site Epsilon Monorail
  3. Jurdistiction over Site Epsilon is absolute and falls solely to the Mobile Task Forces
    1. MTF may order reinforcements from the Security Department and/or its divisions to uphold the integrity of the site in cases of emergency
    2. Attack Drones may protect the Alpha Containment Zone unless ordered to disperse by Crimson-9, or in their absence, any other Operative
    3. The security of the Site Epsilon Monorail is an exception to the transport hub protection obligations of military policemen; it is the duty of Mobile Task Force Crimson-9 to uphold the security of the monorail, or in their absence, Rhino-8
  4. The entirety of Site Epsilon is restricted;
    1. The primary access gate and the round-about is accessible to;
      1. Command Staff
      2. Quality Assurance Testers
      3. Developers
      4. Intelligence Officers
      5. Mobile Task Forces
      6. Personnel accompanying or performing an authorized event
    2. The containment zones are accessible to;
      1. Developers
      2. Mobile Task Forces
      3. Intelligence Officers
      4. Personnel accompanying or performing an authorized event
    3. During tests, personnel must remain in a designated observation area, as instructed by their MTF escort
    4. Deviation from any given instruction or route subjects personnel to prosecution under 1 F.C. ยง 8
  5. The entirety of Site Epsilon is a firearm freezone;
    1. Only the follow individuals may bare arms in Site Epsilon;
      1. Mobile Task Forces
      2. Authorized Security Escorts
      3. Developers
      4. Intelligence Officers
      5. Command Staff
      6. Persons allowed to retain firearms for an approved test hypothesis.
    2. It is the duty of the attending security escort to relinquish persons of firearms, or face punishment under 1 F.C. ยง 21
    3. An individual can not be charged under 5 F.C. ยง 3(5)(b) if a game mechanic stunts their capacity to do so
    4. An individual with security clearance who fails to declare a firearm at a security station is subject to termination and prosecution under 1 F.C. ยง 13
    5. An Class D who fails to declare a firearm at a security station is subject to termination and marking for confinement
  6. Any individual who tresspasses within Site Epsilon may be terminated and prosecuted under 1 F.C.ยง 6
  7. Any individual who enters a containment zone without authorization may be terminated and prosecuted under 1 F.C. ยง 7(c)
  8. Any individual who loiters in the immediate vicinity of Site Epsilon and who fails to disperse after a clear verbal warning may be charged under 1 F.C. ยง 8
